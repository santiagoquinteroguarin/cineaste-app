Cineaste is the most comfortable way to find the proper movie for a relaxed movie night. Here you can easily manage all movies you want to see or you have already seen.

If you want to transfer your movie lists permanently to another device, you can export and import them without any problems.

Open Source Project

Cineaste started in year 2016 with an Android app and since the end of 2017 the SpacePandas are working on the long overdue iOS app. And because we have nothing to hide, Cineaste is open source. That means everybody can see our code and can contribute to the app at GitHub.

We give our best to make the user experience as good as possible, therefore we need your feedback. Leave a comment on the App Store/Play Store or check us out on GitHub.
